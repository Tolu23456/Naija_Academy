3. Isomerism

Compounds with the same molecular formula but different structural arrangements of atoms.

  • Structural Isomerism: Different arrangements of atoms forming different carbon skeletons, positional isomers, or functional group isomers.
  • Stereoisomerism: Same structural formula but different 3D arrangement (Geometric (cis-trans) & Optical).
