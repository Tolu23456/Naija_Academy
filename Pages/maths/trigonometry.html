2. General Triangle Rules

For NON-right angled triangles, use these rules (where side a is opposite angle A, etc.):

  • The Sine Rule: a/sinA = b/sinB = c/sinC (Use when you have a "matching pair" of side and opposite angle).
  • The Cosine Rule: a² = b² + c² - 2bc cosA (Use when you have 3 sides, or 2 sides and the included angle).
  • Area of Triangle: ½ab sinC (Use instead of ½bh when height is unknown).

3. Trigonometric Identities

Key identities heavily tested in WAEC and JAMB:

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
tanθ = sinθ / cosθ
secθ = 1 / cosθ, cosecθ = 1 / sinθ, cotθ = 1 / tanθ
